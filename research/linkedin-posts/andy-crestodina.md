## Post 3 
Link: https://www.linkedin.com/in/andycrestodina/

Beyond your website, what sources does AI use when your buyer researches 
new partners?

...maybe it isn't Reddit after all.

There actually is a way to see which specific sources AI uses to decide 
who to recommend when your future prospect prompts. This little guide 
shows you how to generate a report that shows you those sources and gives 
you a little action plan for what to do next.

It's a multi-step, multi-model process...
1. Predict the prompts that your buyer uses when researching (ChatGPT)
2. Run those prompts in Google (Gemini or AI Mode)
3. Archive the responses and sources (Gemini or AI Mode)
4. Analyze the sources and prioritize next steps (ChatGPT)

Unlike traditional search, it's 100% possible to see which are the most 
important off-site signals. The trick is to pull sources for the most 
important and popular buyer research tool (Google) based on at least 10 
"prompt runs" and then drop them into another AI for analysis (ChatGPT).

It's a bit tedious because you need to copy/paste from one model into 
another 10 times, but the output is fascinating ...and very practical.
